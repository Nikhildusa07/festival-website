About Bathukamma

Bathukamma is not just a festival of flowers, but a celebration of life, togetherness, and cultural pride of Telangana. During this vibrant festival, women create beautiful stacks of seasonal flowers, arranged in concentric layers to form a temple-like shape. They gather in groups, sing traditional folk songs, and dance joyfully around the Bathukamma, expressing devotion to Goddess Gauri. Beyond its religious significance, Bathukamma reflects the deep bond between nature and people, showcasing the respect for mother earth and the seasonal bloom of flowers. It is a symbol of unity, love, and the rich cultural heritage of Telangana, passed down from generation to generation with pride.

Celebrations

  • Women dress in traditional attire
  • Sing Bathukamma songs
  • Dance around the floral arrangement
  • Immersion of Bathukamma in water bodies
