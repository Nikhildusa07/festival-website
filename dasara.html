About Dasara

Dasara, also known as Vijayadashami, is a grand festival that celebrates the eternal victory of good over evil. It signifies the triumph of Goddess Durga over the demon Mahishasura and the victory of Lord Rama over Ravana, symbolizing courage, righteousness, and truth. Across India, Dasara is celebrated with devotion and joy, marked by processions, cultural performances, and the burning of Ravanaâ€™s effigies, reminding people that evil, no matter how powerful, will ultimately fall before the strength of virtue.
